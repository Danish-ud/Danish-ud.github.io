My Projects

Project 1: Restaurant Rating Analysis

Description :

The primary goal of this project is to drive insights from multiple datasets related to restaurants. In this project I performed a detailed analysis of restaurant rating using python(Pandas) for data cleaning and transformation, and power bi for data visualization.

Key Insights :

  • Mexican Cuisine is the most preferred cuisine among customers, but the food offered by the restaurants has a low rating. This highlights a gap between a good taste restaurant based on popularity
  • A significant portion of customers are students and single, suggesting restaurant should tailor offerings and ambience to attract younger people.
  • Restaurants offering valet parking could really enhance the customer satisfaction and rating.
  • Consumers prefer restaurants with separate smoking are. So, restaurants arranging a separate smoking area would enhance their ratings

    Project 2: Telecom Customer Churn Analysis

    Description :

    The primary goal of this project is to identify key factors that lead to customer churn. The goal is to give actionable insights on customer churn to reduce churn and built an interactive Dashboard to show findings.

    Key Insights :

  • Encouraging customers with exciting offers or discounts or exclusive features for switching their package from month-month to one year or two year packages
  • Customer churning under utilize their services like gb used or call allowances, So provide them personalized plans, lower-tier plans for low usage users
  • Churn is more in senior citizens and married users. So, provide personalized family or couple plans to boost retention among users.
  • Churn is more on male customers. So, Conduct surveys with targetted male customers eto identify potential service gaps.
